Notes on writing #

  • Specify (really specify) the goal (or purpose or motivation) and audience
    • Mention them explicitly in the post
    • Keep the content focused on the goal (avoid unnecessary details) and have the audience in mind (tailor the writing to its existing knowledge – keep the level of expertise stable)
  • The content should contain these parts:
    • Introduction – State the context (topics, related posts, …), explain what will be the result of reading the article, set the expectations
    • Middle – Just write the content
    • Conclusion – Give a quick summary what has been learned, inspire the reader to an action
  • Add visual content – it gives a break to the reader, might be funny or provides an illustrative example
  • Keep it simple and easy-to-read
  • Provide strong, concise motivations for everything you want to talk about.
  • Make it easy to skim (headings, strong first paragraphs).
